Coco Chanel's Early Life

August 19, 1883

Birth

Coco Chanel was born on August 19, 1883, in Saumur, France to her parents Albert Chanel and Jeanne Devolle. She also had 5 siblings, and her family lived in poverty for most of her life.

1902

Start Of Her New Life

At the ripe age of 18, Chanel sturck out on her own ot become a seamstress-- this is where it all started. During this time, she also worked as a cafe singer.

1910

Career Finance

After pursuing her singing career, and recieving the origin of her name "Coco", she met Etienne Baslan, and he continued to finance her first stores.

1913

First Boutique

During this time, she opened her first store in Deauville, where she introduced a sportswear collection. This particluar collection was a new revolution for th fashion world.

1910

More Stores

Chanel continued to open her own stores, and opened another one in Paris at 31 Rue Cambon.

1921

Chanel No. 5

During the roaring twenties, she established herself as one of the most influential fashion houses out there. Chanel No. 5 was only one of the first prefumes to come, as she released many more during this decade.

1926

The LBD

The LBD, Little Black Dress, made its entrance in 1926. The simplicty of her new deign challened and revolutionized fashion.

1931

Hollywood

Samuel Goldwyn made her an offer she couldn't refuse, she was going to be able to make million so dollars. She was going to design dresses for famous movie stars. She later realised she had a distate for Hollywood cultuer though.

1954

Coco Comes Back

After her time in Switzerland during the war, Coco reurned to Paris in 1954. She was attmepting to bring back her old style, and reintroduce old fashion. She made many iconic fhasion items, and changd the fashion industry forever.

1971

Rest in Peace

On January 10, 1971, Mademoiselle Chanel passed away. She worked until the last minute of her life and enjoyed it. Her story and fhasion will always create an impact on the world today.
